This site is the self-hosted demo of the **zola-astroplate** Zola theme. It builds the theme
against its own templates and content so the theme can be previewed without a separate consuming
site.

See the [project on GitHub](https://github.com/worrydont/zola-astroplate) for source, docs, and
installation instructions.
